Message from Chairman & Managing Director :

Message from Chairman

Considering infrastructural expansion of the nation & experience in cement trade since long, I realized the necessities of quality cement in our country which encouraged me to set up a Cement factory at the southern part of Bangladesh. In this view we are producing best quality Cement with the highly skilled team effort. Since 2002 Olympic Cement Ltd. has been on an epic journey towards business excellence and our promise to deliver the best to valued customers.

Our Companies logo “Anchor” depicts its vision to become “The symbol of Dependency” not only to its discerning customers but also towards its employees and business partners Our management is highly deserved to gratify our customers by eminence product and services. Satisfying the users Anchor Cement has now won the heart of all levels of customers all over Bangladesh & abroad. We are the proud exporter of cement, where the product has been received with dignity for its high quality.

We are proud of our nation, and also proud of Olympic Cement. I congratulate & appreciate our Valued Customers, Patrons and all Employees.

Kind blessing,

Julia Rahman
Chairman
